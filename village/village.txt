&village7=<font face="Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="14">On the edge of the village is an area of woodland, which is not wild, but carefully managed. The villagers regularly cut back, or <font color="#660000"><u><a href="javascript:openGlos('glossary/coppice.html','glos')">coppice</a></u></font>, the trees so that the new growth is straight and tall.
